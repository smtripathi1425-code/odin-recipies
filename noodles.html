Schezwan Noodles Recipe

Description

So here we are going to delve into the chinese cuisine and preapre one of its most relished dish, that is Schezwan Noodles

Ingridients

  • 200g Hakka Noodles
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped ginger, 1 tbsp garlic, and 1 freshly chopped green chili
  • 3 tbsp Schezwan Sauce
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1.5 cup mixed and sliced veggies
  • 1 tsp soy sauce, 1 tsp vinegar and salt to taste

Steps

  1. Boil and Prepare Noodles
    1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
    2. Add noodles and cook according to instructions
    3. Drain the water and rinse immediately with cold water after 5-7 mins and add some oil to prevent noodles from sticking together.
  2. Saute aromatics and veggies
    1. Heat your oil in large pan
    2. Add chopped ginger, garlic and green chilies and saute for 30 seconds untill fragrant
    3. Add the sliced veggies and stir fry continuously on high heat for 5-7 mins
  3. Toss and serve
    1. Reduce heat to medium. Add the Schezwan sauce, soy sauce,vinegar and salt to the veggies and mix well
    2. Add prepared noodles to wok. toss everything for 3 mins untill noodles are evenly coared and heated through
    3. Garnish with green parts of spring onions and serve well
