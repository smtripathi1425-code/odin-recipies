Biriyani Recipe

Description

So here we are going to delve into the Indian cuisine and preapre one of its most relished dish, that is Biriyani

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cup Basmati Rice (soaked for 20 mins)
  • 200g paneer cubes
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1.5 cup mixed and finely chopped veggies
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste, 3 tsp Biriyani masala, 1/2 cup yogurt and salt

Steps

  1. Par boil the Rice
    1. Boil your soaked basmati rice in salted water with a bay leaf and cardamom until 70% cooked
    2. Ensure rice still has a bite
    3. Drain the water and set aside
  2. Cook paneer and veggies' gravy
    1. Saute sliced onions in oilive oil until golden brown
    2. Add chopped ginger garlic paste and green chilies and saute for 30 seconds untill fragrant
    3. Add the sliced veggies, paneer, biriyani masala, yogurt and stir fry continuously on high heat for 5 mins till a thick gravy forms
  3. Layer and Dum Steam
    1. Layer the par boiled rice over paneer gravy in heavy bottom pot
    2. Top with mint leaves and a spoonful of ghee, seal the lid and steam on low heat for 10-15 minutes
    3. Garnish with green parts of spring onions and serve well
